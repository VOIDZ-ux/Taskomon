## What is Taskomon?

Taskomon is a free, privacy-first habit and task tracker where your consistency directly affects the health of your virtual pet — your **Mon**.

Hit your weekly goals and your Mon thrives. Neglect them and it gets sick. It's that simple.

No account required. No server. No ads. Everything lives on your device.

---

## Features

- **Habits** — set a weekly goal for each habit and track daily completions
- **Tasks** — commit to tasks for the day; missed tasks affect your completion rate
- **Pantry** — store tasks you're not ready to commit to yet, with no penalty
- **Virtual pet (Mon)** — hatches after your first successful week and reacts to your progress in real time
- **Completion rate** — weekly percentage that determines your Mon's health, visible in the calendar
- **Local backup** — export and import your progress as a JSON file
- **Offline-first** — works with no internet connection
- **PWA** — installable on Android, iPhone, and desktop directly from the browser, no app store needed
- **8 languages** — English, Italian, and more
- **Privacy by design** — no accounts, no tracking, no cloud, your data never leaves your device

## How to install

Taskomon is a Progressive Web App — no download needed.

**On Android (Chrome):**
1. Open [voidz-ux.github.io/Taskomon](https://voidz-ux.github.io/Taskomon/) in Chrome
2. Tap the menu → **Add to Home Screen**

**On iPhone (Safari):**
1. Open [voidz-ux.github.io/Taskomon](https://voidz-ux.github.io/Taskomon/) in Safari
2. Tap the share icon → **Add to Home Screen**

**On desktop:**
Just open the link in your browser. Chrome and Edge will offer an install button in the address bar.

Your Mon starts as an egg. After your **first complete week** above your chosen completion threshold, it hatches.

From there, your Mon has four states:

| State | Condition |
|-------|-----------|
| 🥚 Egg | First week not yet complete |
| 😊 Healthy | Weekly completion ≥ threshold |
| 😴 Sleeping | No activity for 3+ hours |
| 🤒 Ill | Week ended below threshold |

Your Mon reacts in real time — complete a task and it animates immediately.

- No internet connection required
- All data stored locally on your device
- Export/import via JSON backup file
